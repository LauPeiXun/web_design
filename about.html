About Us

We stand for something bigger than sneakers.
We champion those who are fearlessly driven by their passions.
To achieve that, the SNAKERS brand is based on the very values that make an excellent style.
We trust our instincts to make unexpected choices that push fashion forward.
Every job at SNAKERS, is grounded in a team-first mindset, cultivating culture of motivation and shared purpose to leave and enduring impact, which we want to fulfil everyone’s needs on their own taste for sneakers.


Our missions

We have a clear mission: To be the best sneakers brand in the world.
Every day, we come to work to create and sell the best products in the world, and to offer the best service and consumer experience, and to do it all in a sustainable way.
We are the best when we are the credible, inclusive, and sustainable leader in our industry. Our products are the perfect blend of function and fashion, giving you the performance technology, you need and the style you want.
Our associates around the world bring these goals to life with their high-level skills and creativity. While we offer the best product on the market, we’re also equally committed to giving back.
So, while we look to succeed, we believe in ensuring others have the same opportunity. As the saying goes: “Sneakers can do a lot more than add to your outfit’s swag. They can also tell a story.”
