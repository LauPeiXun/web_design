PRODUCTS & BRANDS

Products on the SNAKERS site runs in UK sizes. Majority of the products comes with product measurements located at the bottom of the size guide. If available, we recommend you to refer to it to help you select a more appropriate fit.
Please bear in mind that some products may run in different size scales.

Where can I buy SNAKERS's products ? +

You can only buy our products on our website

Can I submit an idea(product / design idea) to SNAKERS for evaluation ? +

Yes. Our policy is accept unsolicited submissions of designs, inventions or ideas from persons external to SNAKERS. We appreciate your interest in our company and seek your cooperation by asking you submit any ideas to SNAKERS.

What can I do about an SNAKERS product I bought that is defective? +

If you are dissatisfied with the quality of your SNAKERS product, please contact our online shop. There, your claim will be discussed competently with you and, if the claim is justified, the purchased item will be replaced. It is our aim to help you in a fast and uncomplicated way if you have a justified claim. If you have ordered the product online, please see our Return Policy for detailed information.

Care instructions for shoes +

Mix warm water and a small amount of laundry detergent to create a slightly soapy mixture. Apply a small amount of the water/detergent mixture to a sponge, soft cloth or soft brush and clean the affected areas. Apply a small amount of warm water to another sponge or soft cloth to remove any excess detergent. Dry the shoes at room temperature. Please do not put the shoes close to a heater.

SHIPPING & DELIVERY

Will I receive any order confirmation after placing an order ? +

An order confirmation with the order summary will be sent to you via EMAIL right after you have placed your order with a successful transaction.

When will I receive my order ? +

You will get an automated message once your order has been shipped out. The delivery times take between 5-7 working days.

How can I track my order ? +

You will be able to track your order through our website. If you are not able to find the tracking number of your parcel, please contact our Customer Service Team and we will be happy to check upon the status of your delivery.

What do I have to do if have received only part of my order ? +

Please contact us as soon as you have received the incomplete order so we can investigate and assist you.

How can I change delivery address/phone number/recipient for existing order ? +

Changes in delivery address/phone number/recipient of the items is not allowed after the order has been successfully made. Kindly ensure all the information is correct prior placing an order.

RETURN

How can I return products purchased online ? +

Merchandise purchased (new, unused, unwashed, unaltered or defective) with tags attached may be returned within 14 days of receipt of your order. Please contact our Customer Service Team for a return label.

When will I receive the money for my returned goods? +

Refund will be made within 14 working days of the returned items being received.

What is the exchange policy for clothes bought online? +

No exchanges are allowed at the moment, you can request for a refund by emailing our Customer Service team at email them.

How can I cancel item ? +

Once your order is placed, we will not be able to cancel the order as it will be processed immediately for fulfillment. For more information, please contact our Customer Service Representative

What can I do if I have not received any refund of my returned goods ? +

Please contact our Customer Service Team and we will be happy to rectify the situation for you.

How do I receive my refund ? +

Refund will be made to your original mode of payment within 14 working days of the returned items being received. This includes credit cards as well
